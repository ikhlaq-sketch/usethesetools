What Do These Scores Mean?

Flesch Reading Ease (0‑100): Higher scores mean easier readability. 60‑70 is standard for adult readers.

Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level: U.S. grade level required to understand the text. Score 8 = 8th grade level.

Gunning Fog Index: Estimates years of formal education needed. Lower is better for general audiences.

FAQs

For general content, aim for Flesch Reading Ease 60+ and Grade Level 8 or lower.
