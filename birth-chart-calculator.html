Understanding Your Big Three

Sun sign: Your core identity and ego. It's the sign you likely already know.
Moon sign: Your emotional inner world. It governs how you feel and react.
Rising sign (Ascendant): Your social mask – how others see you. Requires birth time for accuracy.

This calculator gives approximate results. For a full birth chart with houses and aspects, professional software is needed. But your big three is a great start to understanding yourself.

FAQs

What if I don't know my birth time?
Use 12:00 noon. Your rising sign may be inaccurate, but sun and moon will be correct.
Why do I need timezone?
Rising sign depends on your location's offset from UTC. The calculator uses it for better accuracy.
Is my data stored?
No – all local.
