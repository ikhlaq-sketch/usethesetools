What Is a Life Path Number?

In numerology, your life path number is the most important number in your chart. It is calculated from your full birth date and represents the direction your life will take, including challenges, opportunities, and innate traits.

How to Calculate Yours

Simply add the digits of your birth date until you get a single digit (except 11, 22, 33 which are master numbers). Our calculator does it instantly.

FAQs

Yes, they are synonyms in Western numerology.
