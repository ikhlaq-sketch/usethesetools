What Are Safe Zones?

TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts all have UI elements (like buttons, captions, and usernames) that overlay the bottom and right sides of videos. The "safe zone" is the area where your text/graphics won't be hidden. This tool visualizes those zones so you can adjust your designs accordingly.

FAQs

Which platforms are supported?
TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts – the three major short‑form video platforms.
Are the overlays accurate?
We use common safe‑area guidelines from each platform: top 20% for text, bottom 25% for buttons, etc. Always test on real devices.
