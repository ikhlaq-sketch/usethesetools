How to Create a Personalized Birthday Card

Our free birthday card maker lets you design a custom card in three simple steps. First, enter the recipient’s name – this will appear prominently in an elegant serif font. Second, write a short message (keep it under 60 characters for best layout). Third, choose from four carefully designed themes: Cherry Blossom (pink/gold), Ocean Breeze (blue/teal), Sunset Glow (orange/purple), or Emerald Forest (green/gold). Click “Generate Card” to see your creation. The card includes gradient backgrounds, themed balloons, a festive ribbon banner, and subtle confetti. Once you’re satisfied, click “Download PNG” to save the card as a high‑resolution image. You can then share it via WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, or print it out. No account, no signup, no watermarks – completely free and private.

Tips for Creating Memorable Birthday Cards

  • Keep it short: A short, heartfelt message often has more impact than a long paragraph. Our card is optimized for 1–2 lines.
  • Match the theme to the person: Pink/cherry for romantic or feminine tastes, blue/ocean for calm personalities, sunset for energetic individuals, green/forest for nature lovers.
  • Use the subtitle field: Add “From the whole family” or a date to make the card feel more complete.
  • Share immediately: After downloading, send it in a chat or post to your Instagram story – digital cards are eco‑friendly and arrive instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use these cards commercially?
Yes, generated images are yours to use freely, even for small business sending digital birthday cards to clients. Attribution is appreciated but not required.
Is my personal information stored?
No – everything stays in your browser. We do not have a server, database, or any tracking. The card is drawn on a canvas element locally, and when you download, it never leaves your device.
Can I add more text or change fonts?
Currently, the card is designed for a short name, a short message, and an optional subtitle. This ensures the layout stays clean and elegant. For longer messages, consider using the subtitle field or sending a separate note.
Does this work on mobile phones?
Yes, the tool is fully responsive. You can create and download cards on your phone. The canvas automatically scales to fit your screen. On some phones, the download may save to your camera roll.
What resolution is the downloaded PNG?
The canvas is 550×550 pixels, which is perfect for social media sharing. It prints well at small sizes (e.g., 4"×4"). For larger prints, you can scale up in an image editor.
