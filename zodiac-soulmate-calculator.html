What Is a Zodiac Soulmate?

In astrology, a soulmate isn't necessarily your sun sign opposite. It's about complementary elements and planetary energies. Fire signs (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius) often match best with other Fire or Air signs. Earth signs (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn) match with Earth or Water. Our predictor uses elemental compatibility and traditional astrological affinities to rank your top 3 soulmate signs.

How We Calculate Compatibility

Each combination is scored based on element harmony, modality (Cardinal, Fixed, Mutable), and planetary rulership. For example, Aries (Mars) and Leo (Sun) share Fire, making them passionate matches. Libra and Gemini (both Air) communicate effortlessly.
