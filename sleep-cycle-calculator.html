Why 90‑Minute Sleep Cycles?

Sleep cycles consist of light sleep, deep sleep, and REM. Each cycle lasts about 90 minutes. Waking up in the middle of a cycle causes grogginess (sleep inertia). This calculator suggests bedtimes or wake times that align with cycle endings, so you feel refreshed.

Age affects total sleep need: teens need 8‑10 hours, adults 7‑9, seniors 7‑8. Our recommendations adjust automatically.

FAQs

How accurate is this?
Very accurate for most people. Individual variations exist, but it's a proven method to reduce morning grogginess.
What about sleep onset time?
Most people fall asleep within 15 minutes. The calculator assumes you fall asleep quickly. Add 15‑20 minutes to bedtime if you take longer.
Is my data stored?
No – all processing is local.
