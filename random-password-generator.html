Why Use a Random Password Generator?

Weak passwords are the #1 cause of account breaches. Our generator creates cryptographically strong passwords using browser‑native randomness. You can customize length and character sets. All processing stays in your browser – we never see or store your passwords.

For maximum security, use a password manager to store generated passwords. Never reuse passwords across different sites.

FAQs

Are these passwords truly random?
Yes – we use crypto.getRandomValues() when available, which is the highest standard of randomness in browsers.
Is my password stored anywhere?
No. Everything stays on your screen. We don't log, save, or transmit anything.
What length should I choose?
16 characters is secure for most uses. For high‑value accounts, use 24+ characters.
