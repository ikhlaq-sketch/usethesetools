Privacy Policy

We don't collect, store, or share your personal data. Ever.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At UseTheseTools, your privacy is not an afterthought – it's the foundation. All our tools run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No data is ever sent to our servers, and we don't use cookies or tracking pixels to identify you.

What Data We Collect

None. We deliberately do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Input you type into calculators (past lives, passwords, birth dates, etc.)
  • Uploaded files (images for QR codes, JSON for formatting, etc.)
  • IP addresses or browsing history

Everything you do on this site stays inside your own device. Once you close the tab, it's gone.

Third‑Party Services

We may display advertisements via Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. However, you can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings. We do not control those cookies and have no access to any data collected by ad networks.

Children's Privacy

Our tools are not directed at children under 13, but we welcome everyone. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever modify this policy, the "last updated" date below will change. We will never betray your trust by starting to collect data without clear notice.
