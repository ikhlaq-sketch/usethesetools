Privacy Policy

We don't collect, store, or share your personal data. Ever.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At UseTheseTools, your privacy is not an afterthought – it's the foundation. All our tools run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No data is ever sent to our servers, and we do not use persistent tracking cookies (except for Google AdSense advertisements, which are clearly disclosed). We do not create user profiles, and we never have access to the information you enter into our tools – whether it’s your birth date for a numerology calculator, a password in our strength checker, or JSON code that you format.

What Data We Collect

None. We deliberately do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Input you type into calculators (past lives, passwords, birth dates, etc.)
  • Uploaded files (images for QR codes, JSON for formatting, etc.)
  • IP addresses or browsing history
  • Any analytics data – we do not use Google Analytics or similar tracking scripts

Everything you do on this site stays inside your own device. Once you close the tab, it's gone. You can verify this by opening your browser’s Developer Tools (F12) and watching the Network tab – you will see zero HTTP requests to our servers when you use any tool (except the initial page load and optional AdSense ads).

Third‑Party Services – Google AdSense

We may display advertisements via Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. These cookies are set by Google, not by us. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings. You can also disable cookies entirely in your browser, but that may affect the relevance of ads you see across the web.

We do not control those cookies and have no access to any data collected by ad networks. For more information, please review Google’s Advertising Privacy Policy.

If you prefer not to see any ads, you can use an ad blocker – but please consider that ad revenue helps keep our tools free for everyone.

Cookies

We do not set any first‑party cookies. However, Google AdSense may set third‑party cookies for ad personalization. These are optional – you can disable them via your browser settings or the Google Ad Settings page. Our tools themselves do not require cookies to function.

Children's Privacy

Our tools are not directed at children under 13, but we welcome everyone. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, regardless of age. Since we collect no data, there is no risk of violating COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act). Parents and guardians can rest assured that their children can use our tools safely without leaving any digital footprint.

Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, and Other Privacy Laws)

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, rectify, delete, or port. You have the right to use our site without any data collection whatsoever. If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), California (USA), or any other jurisdiction with data protection laws, you can be confident that your privacy is fully respected – we do not even have a database to store your information.

If you have any questions about how we handle your data (or rather, how we don’t), please contact us. We will respond promptly.

Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of a data breach from our side. Your inputs stay on your own device and are never exposed to network vulnerabilities. The only data exchanged is the static HTML/CSS/JS files that make up the site – served over HTTPS for secure transmission.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever modify this policy, the “last updated” date below will change. We will never betray your trust by starting to collect data without clear notice. Any future changes will be highlighted on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed.
