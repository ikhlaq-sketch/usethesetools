What Is a Rising Sign (Ascendant)?

The rising sign is the zodiac sign that was on the eastern horizon at your exact moment of birth. It changes every two hours. Your rising sign governs your outward behavior, physical appearance, and how others perceive you. While your sun sign is your core self, your rising sign is your social mask.

Why Birth Time Is Critical

Even a 15‑minute difference can change your rising sign. This calculator uses an approximate algorithm based on time of day and longitude approximation (using timezone). For extreme precision, professional software is needed, but our tool gives accurate results for most locations.

FAQs

What if I don't know my birth time?
Use 12:00 noon as a rough estimate, but the result may be inaccurate. Rising sign requires exact time.
Does location affect rising sign?
Yes, but we approximate using timezone offset. For best results, use your local timezone.
Is my data stored?
No – everything is calculated locally.
