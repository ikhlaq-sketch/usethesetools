FAQs

Is there any limit on the data size?
QR codes have a practical limit of about 3,000 characters. Very long text may produce a dense code, but it will still be readable by most scanners.
Can I use this for commercial purposes?
Yes – generated QR codes are yours to use freely, even for business.
Is my data safe?
Yes – the QR code is generated entirely in your browser. No server is involved.
