We built UseTheseTools to be a free, private, no‑signup resource for everyone. Your feedback directly shapes what we build next. If you need a calculator or utility that doesn’t exist yet, tell us – we often add new tools within days based on user suggestions.

We also appreciate bug reports (no tool is perfect) and general feedback. Every message is read by a human, and we reply promptly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?
We aim to reply within 48 hours, often sooner. For tool suggestions, we may take a few extra days to evaluate feasibility.
Can I suggest a new tool?
Absolutely! Use the contact form and select 'Tool suggestion' as the subject. We review every idea and often build the most requested ones within days.
Do you charge for tool suggestions or custom tools?
No, all tools on UseTheseTools are completely free forever. If we build your suggestion, it will be added to the site at no cost to anyone.
Is my personal data safe when I contact you?
Yes. We only use your email to reply. We never sell or share your contact information. The form submission is processed via a secure service (Formspree).
Can I report a bug or error in a tool?
Yes – please select 'Bug report' and include the tool name and a description of the issue. We fix bugs within 24–48 hours in most cases.
Do you accept guest posts or backlink requests?
We do not accept paid guest posts. However, if you run a high‑quality tools directory and wish to list us, feel free to reach out.
